FRAMEWORK BUG FOUND + FIXED MID-ITERATION
-----------------------------------------
  Iteration 1 + iteration 2 combined exposed a latent bug: with
  use_lonely_cell_clauses=True and the tighter upper_bound the solver
  drove a(1) to 2 (wrongly PROVED) instead of the correct 1. Root
  cause: the lonely-cell clause "every selected cell has a selected
  neighbour" is invalid when the optimal container is a single cell
  (the n=1 case). Verifier 1 (geometric set-inclusion) flagged the
  disagreement at n=1 during the incremental output check.

  Fix (commit 0edd24c1, 2026-04-23): sat_utils/frameworks/container.py
  adds `and ctx.n >= 2` guards to both CNF build sites before calling
  lonely_cell_clauses. 36/36 framework tests pass. Both verifiers
  agree on every n after the fix.
